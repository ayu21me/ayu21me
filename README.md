### 🚀 Featured Projects

#### 📈 [Digital Procurement Analytics](https://github.com/ayu21me/digital-procurement-analytics)
*An end-to-end data project focused on optimizing corporate supply chain efficiency.*
- **Problem:** Inconsistent vendor lead times and unoptimized procurement spending.
- **Solution:** Developed a Python-based analysis tool to track Supplier Performance and Spend Categorization.
- **Outcome:** Identified key areas for cost-saving and automated the generation of procurement reports.
- **Stack:** Python, Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn.

---

#### 👥 [Customer Segmentation using RFM Analysis](https://github.com/ayu21me/rfm-customer-segmentation)
*A customer analytics project focused on identifying high-value users and churn risks.*

- **Problem:** Businesses struggle to identify their most valuable customers and detect churn risk.
- **Solution:** Performed **RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) analysis** on **540K+ e-commerce transactions** to segment customers based on purchasing behavior.
- **Outcome:** Identified key customer segments such as **Champions, Loyal Customers, Potential Loyalists, At Risk, and Lost Customers**, enabling targeted marketing strategies.
- **Stack:** Python, Pandas, NumPy, Power BI.
